Nimbus Vault — Pricing and Plans

Nimbus Vault offers three plans, billed monthly or annually (annual billing saves 20%).

1. Home Plan — $6.99/month
   - 500 GB of backup storage
   - Up to 3 linked devices
   - 90-day version history
   - Email support only, 48-hour response time

2. Business Plan — $18.99/month per user, minimum 3 users
   - 2 TB of backup storage per user, pooled across the team
   - Up to 10 linked devices per user
   - 180-day version history
   - Priority chat support, 4-hour response time
   - Includes shared team folders

3. Enterprise Plan — custom pricing, contact sales
   - Unlimited backup storage
   - Unlimited linked devices
   - Unlimited version history
   - 60-second sync intervals
   - Dedicated account manager and 1-hour SLA support
   - Single sign-on (SSO) and SCIM provisioning included

A free trial of the Business Plan is available for 14 days, no credit card required. Storage overages on the Home Plan are billed at $2.50 per additional 100 GB block, charged at the end of the billing cycle.

Nonprofit organizations receive a 30% discount on the Business Plan when verified through TechSoup.
