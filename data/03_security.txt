Nimbus Vault — Security and Compliance

All files are encrypted client-side using AES-256 before leaving the device, and again in transit using TLS 1.3. Aurorix Systems never has access to unencrypted file contents; encryption keys are derived from the user's master password using PBKDF2 with 600,000 iterations and are never transmitted to Aurorix servers.

Because encryption is client-side, Nimbus Vault cannot recover a user's files if the master password is lost. Business and Enterprise plans offer an optional "Key Escrow" feature where a designated account administrator can hold a recovery key, but this is disabled by default and must be explicitly enabled per user.

Compliance certifications held by Aurorix Systems as of 2026:
- SOC 2 Type II
- ISO/IEC 27001
- HIPAA compliance available on the Enterprise plan only, requires a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
- GDPR-compliant data processing, with EU data residency available on Business and Enterprise plans (data centers in Frankfurt and Dublin)

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory on all Business and Enterprise accounts as of January 2026. It is optional but strongly recommended on Home accounts. Supported 2FA methods are TOTP authenticator apps and hardware security keys (FIDO2/WebAuthn); SMS-based 2FA was deprecated in September 2025 and is no longer supported.
